# AI Content Generator - Job Posting

## Position Overview

We are seeking an experienced AI Content Generator proficient with ChatGPT, Claude, or other advanced AI applications to create high-quality, SEO-optimized website content. This is a **long-term project** with the opportunity to work on multiple websites, generating hundreds of pages of engaging, informative content.

---

## Responsibilities

### Primary Duties:
- Generate website content based on provided themes and specific prompts
- Create content in batches (1-20 pages per batch, depending on your preference and system stability)
- Populate multiple websites with content-rich material
- Research and identify potential subcategories within main categories
- Think strategically about content structure and organization
- Check generated content into provided GitHub repositories
- Ensure content quality, coherence, and SEO optimization
- Work collaboratively to refine content based on feedback

### Content Types:
- Informational articles
- Category pages
- Topic-specific content pages
- Research-driven articles
- SEO-optimized web content

### Example Tasks:
- "Generate content page about history of french fries in [specific theme]"
- Create subcategory pages within main categories
- Develop content hierarchies and content maps
- Generate batches of related content pages

---

## Requirements

### Essential Skills:
- ✅ **Proficiency with AI Tools**: Extensive experience with ChatGPT, Claude, GPT-4, or similar AI content generation platforms
- ✅ **Content Writing**: Strong writing and editing skills

- ✅ **Technical Skills**: Basic familiarity with GitHub (or willingness to learn)
- ✅ **SEO Knowledge**: Understanding of SEO best practices for web content
- ✅ **Attention to Detail**: Ability to review and refine AI-generated content
- ✅ **Self-Motivated**: Ability to work independently with minimal supervision
- ✅ **Long-term Commitment**: Looking for someone committed to an ongoing project

### Preferred Skills:
- Experience with content management systems (CMS)
- Understanding of content marketing strategies
- Knowledge of different industries/themes
- Ability to adapt writing style to different themes
- Experience with batch content generation

## Pricing & Compensation

### We are flexible with pricing models:

#### Option 1: Fixed Rate per Page
- **Rate**: ₹150 - ₹500 per page (depending on complexity, length, and research required)
- **Advantages**: Predictable income, clear pricing structure
- **Payment**: Per batch delivery

#### Option 2: Hourly Rate
- **Rate**: ₹300 - ₹800 per hour
- **Advantages**: Fair compensation for research and refinement time
- **Payment**: Weekly or bi-weekly based on logged hours

#### Option 3: Monthly Retainer
- **Rate**: ₹25,000 - ₹60,000 per month
- **Output**: Minimum 50-100 pages per month (negotiable)
- **Advantages**: Steady income, long-term commitment
- **Payment**: Monthly advance or as agreed

#### Option 4: Project-Based Pricing
- **Rate**: Negotiable based on project scope
- **Advantages**: Flexibility for specific content campaigns
- **Payment**: Milestone-based

### Rate Determination Factors:
- Content complexity and research requirements
- Word count per page (typically 800-2000 words)
- Number of subcategories to research
- SEO optimization level required
- Revision rounds included

### Payment Terms:
- Payment via UPI, Bank Transfer, or preferred method
- Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly payments (negotiable)
- Clear invoicing and documentation
