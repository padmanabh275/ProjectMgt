# Pricing Guidelines for AI Content Generator Position

## Market Research - INR Rates (India)

### Content Writing Industry Rates (2024):
- **Entry Level**: ₹100-200/page
- **Mid Level**: ₹200-400/page
- **Experienced**: ₹400-600/page
- **Expert/SEO Specialist**: ₹600-1000+/page

### Hourly Rates:
- **Junior Writer**: ₹200-400/hour
- **Mid-Level**: ₹400-600/hour
- **Senior/Expert**: ₹600-1000+/hour

### AI-Assisted Content Rates:
- **Basic AI Generation**: ₹150-300/page (minimal editing)
- **AI + Human Refinement**: ₹300-500/page (quality editing)
- **AI + Research + SEO**: ₹450-700/page (full service)

## Recommended Pricing Structure

### For Your Project (AI Content Generation):

#### **Best Option: Fixed Rate per Page**

**Tier 1 - Simple Content** (800-1200 words, basic research):
- Rate: ₹250-350 per page
- Includes: AI generation, basic editing, formatting

**Tier 2 - Standard Content** (1200-1600 words, moderate research):
- Rate: ₹350-500 per page
- Includes: AI generation, research, editing, subcategory identification

**Tier 3 - Premium Content** (1600-2000+ words, extensive research):
- Rate: ₹500-700 per page
- Includes: Deep research, multiple subcategories, SEO optimization, thorough editing

**Bonus**: ₹50-100 extra for complex subcategory research

#### **Alternative: Hourly Rate**

**For Content Generation:**
- ₹500-700/hour (covers AI generation + refinement)

**For Research & Planning:**
- ₹400-600/hour (subcategory identification, content planning)

**Blended Rate:**
- ₹450-650/hour (mixed work)

#### **Monthly Retainer (Recommended for Long-term)**

**Basic Package** (50-60 pages/month):
- ₹30,000-40,000/month
- Simple to standard content
- 2-3 revisions included

**Standard Package** (75-85 pages/month):
- ₹40,000-50,000/month
- Mix of standard and premium content
- 3-4 revisions included

**Premium Package** (100+ pages/month):
- ₹50,000-65,000/month
- All content types
- Unlimited revisions within reason
- Priority support

## Payment Terms Recommendations

### Frequency:
- **Per Batch**: Pay after each batch delivery (1-20 pages)
- **Weekly**: Pay weekly for completed work
- **Bi-weekly**: Pay every 2 weeks
- **Monthly**: Pay monthly retainer

### Payment Methods:
- UPI (instant)
- Bank Transfer (NEFT/IMPS)
- Other preferred methods

### Payment Timing:
- **Advance**: 25-50% advance for monthly retainers
- **Milestone**: Pay on batch completion
- **Net 7-15**: Pay within 7-15 days of delivery

---

## Quality Considerations

### What Affects Pricing:

1. **Word Count**: Longer content = higher price
2. **Research Depth**: More research = higher price
3. **Subcategories**: Complex hierarchies = higher price
4. **SEO Level**: Advanced SEO = higher price
5. **Revision Rounds**: More revisions = higher price
6. **Turnaround Time**: Rush jobs = premium

### Quality Expectations:

- **Minimum**: Grammatically correct, coherent, on-topic
- **Standard**: Well-structured, researched, SEO-friendly
- **Premium**: In-depth research, multiple sources, expert-level content

## Sample Calculation

### Scenario: 50 Pages per Month

**Option 1 - Fixed Rate:**
- 30 simple pages @ ₹300 = ₹9,000
- 15 standard pages @ ₹450 = ₹6,750
- 5 premium pages @ ₹600 = ₹3,000
- **Total: ₹18,750/month**

**Option 2 - Hourly (assuming 2 hours/page average):**
- 50 pages × 2 hours = 100 hours
- 100 hours × ₹550 = **₹55,000/month**

**Option 3 - Monthly Retainer:**
- **₹35,000-45,000/month** (negotiated fixed rate)

## Negotiation Tips

### For You (Employer):
- Start with lower end of range, offer increase after trial
- Offer monthly retainer for better rates
- Provide clear guidelines to reduce revision needs
- Consider performance bonuses

### For Candidate:
- Highlight AI expertise and experience
- Show portfolio and quality samples
- Propose fair rate based on market research
- Negotiate for monthly retainer if committing long-term
- Ask for clear scope to avoid scope creep
